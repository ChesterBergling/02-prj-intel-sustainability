Intel Timeline

1968 — Intel Founded

Intel was founded in 1968, marking the beginning of the modern semiconductor industry.

1971 — First Microprocessor

Intel introduced the world’s first commercial microprocessor, revolutionizing computing.

1978 — Intel 8086

The Intel 8086 microprocessor launched, laying the foundation for the x86 architecture.

1985 — Intel Innovation

Intel continued to push forward with powerful processors, shaping the future of PCs.
